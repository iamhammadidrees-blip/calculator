Calculator Script Workflow

Functions to reuse

.

- getNumber(message): prompts until a valid float is entered.

- getOperator(): prompts until one of +, -, *, / is chosen.

- calculate(a, b, op): returns the arithmetic result.

- displayResult(a, b, op, result): shows the final expression and value.

- main(): glues everything together in a clear sequence.

With this structure, each piece does one thing and you can easily swap out displayResult for an alert or a DOM update without touching your core logic.
